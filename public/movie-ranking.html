The Best Movies According to Katie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Princess Diaries

This is a sweet movie, very well done, and comforting in a way.

The Matrix

I think this movie, the series actually, was well before its time. I rewatch it every so often because I find something else cool about it the older I get.

Legally Blonde

I think I would never get tired of this movie. It is so empowering and just a feel good movie.
